As a native of Flint, Michigan, video games have been a staple of my life. By the age 9, I was convinced that I should be making games. I began writing stories for video games I envisioned, drawing characters and environments, and even created my own music and soundtracks. By 13 I had created my first game, and my love for video games soon became a love for computers in general. I hopes to create great experiences for users everywhere and make my love for computers, games, art, writing, and music, contagious for new generations.
